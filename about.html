About M A Zuha

M A Zuha is a writer and photographer from Dhaka, Bangladesh. He serves as the CEO of the ZuhaWorld Social Impact. Furthermore, he loves to learn and committed to lifelong learning and development.

A Little Introduction About ZuhaWorld Social Impact

ZuhaWorld Social Impact is always one step ahead of the competition. They defined what it means to be an innovator by establishing social responsibility.

We are the most popular social club in Muradpur, Dhaka. We began our journey in 2011 with the slogan, “Maybe you have no friend in your world, but here is ZuhaWorld, who is always ready to say welcome.” We believe in creating a culture of information sharing and constant learning. We bring together local ingenuity and global insights to save lives, advance equity, and secure strong futures.
